Yelp Rating vs. Vader Compound Score



This plot shows the Yelp rating and Vader compound score. The Vader compound score ranges from -1 to 1. We were surprised to see a majority of the compound scores were between 0 and 0.5, which is considered a neutral sentiment. We expected to see negative compound scores which would mean the reviews have a negative sentiment. Unexpectedly, there is no correlation between the Yelp rating and Vader compound score.
