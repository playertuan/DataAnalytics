Summary

The purpose of this project is to analyze the SEC13F filings of Berkshire Hathaway Inc. Berkshire Hathaway Inc. is a holding company run by Warren Buffet. Warren Buffet is a self made billionaire with over $80 billion in assets. As of November 17, 2017, one share of BRK-A stock is $271,400.

The Securities Exchange Commission (SEC) requires institutional investment managers with over $100 million in qualifying assets to file a SEC13F form listing their recent investment holdings. The SEC13F form must be filed within 45 days of the end of each quarter.

Observations:
  • Since 12/31/2016, Berkshire Hathaway has increased shares in Apple. Apple is also the 2nd highest performing stock from 12/31/2016 - 09/30/2017.
  • Since 12/31/2016, Berkshire Hathaway has decreased shares in IBM. IBM is also the 2nd lowest performing stock from 12/31/2016 - 09/30/2017.
  • Since 12/31/2016, Berkshire Hathaway has sold out of Kraft and Coca-Cola.
  • In Q3 2017, Berkshire Hathaway sold off shares in Wells Fargo, IBM, and Charter Communications.
